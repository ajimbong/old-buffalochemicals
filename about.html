About Us

Who We Are

Buffalo Chemicals is a small yet excellent automotive repair service provider, employing the most cuttingedge diagnostic technologies and resources for vehicle maintenance and repairs. We offer the automotive repair market an entirely new approach to automotive repair service practices. Our processes and approach aim to give our customers the best in terms of conduct of technical performance, customer participation and satisfaction. In an industry where customer satisfaction is widely undervalued, we bring into focus an entirely new approach to the maintenance and repair business.

Customer Focus

CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT: Beyond technical excellence, we engage, as a priority, our customers in the repair management process with the aim of getting them to know and better communicate with their vehicles.

REPORTING: We provide complete and concise diagnostic and advisory reports to all our customers and keep long-term reports for all repairs and maintenance works on their vehicles. This is key to enabling our customers have a clear and factual picture of their cars’ health at any given time. It also facilitates their ability to keep track of all maintenance and repair works done on their vehicles extended periods. We achieve this through electronic and archiving and offer of free maintenance booklets to all our registered customers. For corporate institutions, we take a step further with the provision of free fleet management services; involving maintenance and repair tracking and reporting.

Customer Management

Our business model focuses on an association of technical excellence and customer focus. To achieve this objective vehicle admission to Buffalo Chemicals is preferably done on a booking basis. We find this particularly relevant for the purpose of providing our customers with personalized services and for proper technical planning.

Technical Focus

For the purpose of achieving unmatched levels of technical excellence Buffalo Chemicals has made extensive investment in the most globally advanced diagnostic tools, technical repair information and equipment; the idea, being to significantly minimize the possibility of guesswork, excessive and unnecessary customer expenditure.
